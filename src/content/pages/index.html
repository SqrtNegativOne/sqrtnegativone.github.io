    #Hi!

    I am a B.Tech undergrad from Delhi, India.

    I have always found myself fascinated with computers and programming from when I started tinkering around with them. The joy of creation; deep within we all want to create something that is helpful, whether to us or to others. Add puzzle solving and constant learning to the mix and you have a frustrating yet addictive feedback loop that gratifies a very primeval longing in a very modern way.

    It is my lifelong goal to continue chasing that high of looking at a finished product, understanding fully the intricacies of the system (for you only created it) and feeling your brain be molded by it. So I continue to challenge myself in new ways, with new technologies, and new tools.

    In particular, I am interested in backend development and artificial intelligence.

    I can't prove it without solving the unsolvable problem of the Hard Problem of Consciousness, but I promise that I am a real, living, conscious human being, with their own wants, needs, ideas, hopes, and dreams, for themselves, and the world.

    Other than programming itself, my hobbies include reading books, reading long-form essays on the web, and playing puzzle and story based games. If you'd like to know more about me personally, check my dedicated about page. Which doesn't exist... not yet. But it will. Maybe follow me on Github to find out when it happens... ( ͡° ͜ʖ ͡°)
